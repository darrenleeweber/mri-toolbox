Overview

Axial slices are useful for identifying subcortical landmarks. Some landmarks to look for are:

  • caudate nucleus (magenta)
  • claustrum (pink) - sliver lateral to putamen, e.g. slice 0
  • globus pallidus (green)
  • putamen (yellow)
  • quadrigeminal plate (purple)
  • thalamus (red) - poorly defined in T1 scans
  • vermis (ice blue)
  • sylvian fissure (dotted red)
  • superior temproal sulcus (dotted green)
  • inferior temporal sulcus (dotted yellow)

Chris Rorden, 13 October 2002
